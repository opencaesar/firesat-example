Abstract

The Project Ontology defines concepts and properties for describing a project in terms of its stakeholders, concerns, work breakdown, and deliverables. It provides a basis for specialization by domain experts.

3. Aspects

3.1. AssignedElement

An AssignedElement is any model element that can have the isAssignedTo relationship with a Role .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

Subtypes: Delivers, DevelopedElement, Executes, Process, Receives, ReliesOn, Supplies

Relations with target:

  • Role ← HasAssignment → AssignedElement

3.2. DevelopedElement

A DevelopedElement is any model element that manifests DevelopmentState s. Example DevelopedElement s include spacecraft, thermal test facility, and requirements document.

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, AssignedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

Subtypes: mission:Component, Facility, mission:Interface, mission:Junction, Product

Relations with source:

  • DevelopedElement ← Manifests → DevelopmentState

Relations with target:

  • Product ← Describes → DevelopedElement

  • Authority ← ReliesOn → DevelopedElement

3.3. RealizedElement

A RealizedElement is any element that realizes or isRealizedBy another element.

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

Subtypes: mission:Component, mission:Function, mission:Interface, mission:Junction, mission:Mission, Process, Project, WorkPackage

Relations with source:

  • RealizedElement ← Realizes → RealizedElement

Relations with target:

  • RealizedElement ← Realizes → RealizedElement

3.4. SuppliedElement

A SuppliedElement is any model element that an Authority properly supplies . Example SuppliedElement s include Mission and Component .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

Subtypes: mission:Component, mission:Mission, Product

Relations with target:

  • Authority ← Supplies → SuppliedElement

4. Concepts

4.1. Authority

An Authority is any entity that asserts a fact in a model. Example Authority s include Program , Project , and WorkPackage . (The association of an

Authority with its asserted facts requires mechanisms outside the vocabulary of OWL itself, e.g., named graphs. For this reason, there is no object property authorizes . Authorization is conveyed by the organization of statements in a repository.)

Supertypes: base:Package

Subtypes: Program, Project, WorkPackage

Relations with source:

  • Authority ← Authorizes → Authority

  • Authority ← Executes → Process

  • Authority ← Produces → Product

  • Authority ← ReliesOn → DevelopedElement

  • Authority ← Supplies → SuppliedElement

Relations with target:

  • Authority ← Authorizes → Authority

  • Organization ← HasResponsibilityFor → Authority

4.2. Concern

A Concern represents a specific interest that one or more stakeholders have in the successful completion of a Project or Program and its Mission s.

Supertypes: base:AggregatedElement, analysis:ConstrainedElement, base:IdentifiedElement

Subtypes: mission:Objective

Relations with source:

  • Concern ← base:Aggregates → Concern

Relations with target:

  • Concern ← base:Aggregates → Concern

  • Stakeholder ← Represents → Concern

4.3. Deliverable

A Deliverable is the result of successful execution of a Process . It may be an input to or prerequisite for another Process .

Supertypes: base:IdentifiedElement

Subtypes: DevelopmentState

Properties:

  • isRequiredNotLaterThan

Relations with target:

  • InterchangePoint ← Delivers → Deliverable

  • Interchange ← HasParticipant → Deliverable

  • InterchangePoint ← Receives → Deliverable

4.4. DevelopmentState

A DevelopmentState is a kind of Deliverable that represents a defined condition for a DevelopedElement . DevelopmentState s may be unique to the

DevelopedElement ; example DevelopmentState s for a Product include Preliminary, Draft, and Final.

Supertypes: analysis:Characterization, Deliverable

Relations with source:

  • DevelopmentState ← analysis:Characterizes → DevelopedElement

Relations with target:

  • DevelopedElement ← Manifests → DevelopmentState

4.5. Facility

A Facility is any asset (e.g., laboratory, assembly facility, test facility, etc.) whose DevelopmentState may be a Deliverable for a project process.

Supertypes: DevelopedElement, base:IdentifiedElement

4.6. Interchange

An Interchange represents the intended joint behavior of a pair of Process es. It indicates that a Deliverable may be delivered by one Process and received by the other Process .

Supertypes: base:IdentifiedElement

Relations with source:

  • Interchange ← HasParticipant → Deliverable

  • Interchange ← Involves → Process

  • Interchange ← Involves1 → Process

  • Interchange ← Involves2 → Process

4.7. InterchangePoint

Supertypes: base:IdentifiedElement

Relations with source:

  • InterchangePoint ← Delivers → Deliverable

  • InterchangePoint ← Receives → Deliverable

Relations with target:

  • Process ← InterchangesThrough → InterchangePoint

4.8. Organization

An Organization is any entity that isResponsibleFor a Authority .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

Relations with source:

  • Organization ← HasResponsibilityFor → Authority

Relations with target:

  • Person ← BelongsTo → Organization

4.9. Person

A Person corresponds to an individual named person. A Person belongsTo zero or more Organization s.

Supertypes: base:IdentifiedElement

Relations with source:

  • Person ← BelongsTo → Organization

  • Person ← HasRole → Role

4.10. Policy

A Policy represents a specific exercise of authority that binds one or more model elements.

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

Relations with source:

  • Policy ← Binds → mission:SpecifiedElement

4.11. Process

A Process is an activity authorized by a WorkPackage in support of delivering a Component or producing a Product . Examples include design trajectory , conduct trade study , and review subsystem design .

Supertypes: AssignedElement, base:IdentifiedElement, RealizedElement

Relations with source:

  • Process ← InterchangesThrough → InterchangePoint

  • Process ← Invokes → Process

  • Process ← Realizes → Process

Relations with target:

  • Authority ← Executes → Process

  • Process ← Invokes → Process

  • Interchange ← Involves → Process

  • Interchange ← Involves1 → Process

  • Interchange ← Involves2 → Process

  • Process ← Realizes → Process

4.12. Product

A Product is a document or other artifact supplied by a WorkPackage . Example Product s include trade study reports, design documents, and inspection reports.

Supertypes: base:AggregatedElement, DevelopedElement, base:IdentifiedElement, SuppliedElement

Relations with source:

  • Product ← base:Aggregates → Product

  • Product ← Describes → DevelopedElement

Relations with target:

  • Product ← base:Aggregates → Product

  • Role ← Approves → Product

  • Role ← Concurs → Product

  • Role ← Prepares → Product

  • Authority ← Produces → Product

4.13. Program

Supertypes: Authority

4.14. Project

A Project is a kind of Authority that supplies a related set of Mission s in pursuit of a set of Objective s.

Supertypes: Authority, RealizedElement

Relations with source:

  • Project ← Realizes → Project

  • Project ← Supplies → mission:Mission

Relations with target:

  • Project ← Realizes → Project

4.15. Role

A Role corresponds to a set of assignments meant to be filled by a single Person .

Supertypes: base:IdentifiedElement

Relations with source:

  • Role ← Approves → Product

  • Role ← Concurs → Product

  • Role ← DelegatesTo → Role

  • Role ← HasAssignment → AssignedElement

  • Role ← Prepares → Product

Relations with target:

  • Role ← DelegatesTo → Role

  • Person ← HasRole → Role

4.16. Stakeholder

A Stakeholder is a person or organization with a recognized interest in the successful completion of a Project or Program . Example

Stakeholder s include scientists, NASA Directorates, nuclear safety advocates, and the general public.

Supertypes: base:IdentifiedElement

Relations with source:

  • Stakeholder ← Represents → Concern

4.17. WorkPackage

A WorkPackage represents a discrete unit of Project cost, schedule, and activity. WorkPackage s form a hierarchy called the Work Breakdown Structure.

Supertypes: Authority, fse:MassAllocatedElement, fse:MassEstimatedElement, base:Package, RealizedElement

Relations with source:

  • WorkPackage ← Realizes → WorkPackage

  • WorkPackage ← Supplies → mission:Component

Relations with target:

  • WorkPackage ← Realizes → WorkPackage

Target: Product

Reverse Relation: isApprovedBy

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement, HasAssignment

Attributes: InverseFunctional, Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Authority

Target: Authority

Forward Relation: authorizes

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.3. BelongsTo

Attributes:

Source: Person

Target: Organization

Forward Relation: belongsTo A Person belongsTo zero or more Organization s.

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.4. Binds

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Policy

Target: mission:SpecifiedElement

Forward Relation: binds A Policy p binds a SpecifiedElement e if and only if the course of action embodied in p governs the design, analysis, implementation, operation, or other applicable aspects of e .

Reverse Relation: isBoundBy See binds .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.5. Concurs

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Role

Target: Product

Forward Relation: concurs

Reverse Relation: isConcurredBy

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement, HasAssignment

5.6. DelegatesTo

Attributes: InverseFunctional, Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Role

Target: Role

Forward Relation: delegatesTo A Role r 1 delegatesTo a Role r 2 if and only if r 1 gives direction or otherwise exercises authority over r 2 .

Reverse Relation: isDelegateOf See delegatesTo .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.7. Delivers

Attributes: InverseFunctional, Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: InterchangePoint

Target: Deliverable

Forward Relation: delivers A Process p delivers a Deliverable r if and only if the successful execution of p results in availability of r .

Reverse Relation: isDeliveredBy See delivers .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, AssignedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.8. Describes

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Product

Target: DevelopedElement

Forward Relation: describes A Product p describes a DevelopmentElement e if and only if p contains design, analysis, operations, or other information about e .

Reverse Relation: isDescribedBy See describes .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.9. Executes

Attributes: InverseFunctional, Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Authority

Target: Process

Forward Relation: executes An Authority a executes a Process p if and only if a carries out any actions required for successful completion of p .

Reverse Relation: isExecutedBy See executes .

Supertypes: AssignedElement, mission:SpecifiedElement

5.10. HasAssignment

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Role

Target: AssignedElement

Forward Relation: hasAssignment A Role r hasAssignment some AssignedElement e if and only if r has specific cognizance and/or responsibility for e .

Reverse Relation: isAssignedTo See hasAssignment .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

Subtypes: Approves, Concurs, Prepares

5.11. HasParticipant

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Interchange

Target: Deliverable

Forward Relation: hasParticipant

Reverse Relation: participatesIn

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.12. HasResponsibilityFor

Attributes: InverseFunctional

Source: Organization

Target: Authority

Forward Relation: hasResponsibilityFor An Organization o hasResponsibilityFor some Authority a if and only if o provides the actual workforce and execution of the work embodied in a . An example is the relation between a "doing"

Organization and a WorkPackage .

Reverse Relation: isResponsibilityOf See hasResponsibilityFor .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.13. HasRole

Attributes: InverseFunctional, Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Person

Target: Role

Forward Relation: hasRole A Person p hasRole Role r if and only if p acts to execute the assignments of r .

Reverse Relation: isRoleOf See hasRole .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.14. InterchangesThrough

Attributes: InverseFunctional, Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Process

Target: InterchangePoint

Forward Relation: interchangesThrough

Reverse Relation: isInterchangePointFor

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.15. Invokes

Attributes: Irreflexive

Source: Process

Target: Process

Forward Relation: invokes A PerformedElement p invokes a PerformedElement c if and only if the execution of c may be triggered or caused by and within the execution of p .

Reverse Relation: isInvokedBy See invokes .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.16. Involves

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Interchange

Target: Process

Forward Relation: involves

Reverse Relation: isInvolvedIn

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

Subtypes: Involves1, Involves2

Source: Interchange

Target: Process

Forward Relation: involves1

Reverse Relation: isInvolvedIn1

Supertypes: Involves

Attributes: Functional, Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Interchange

Target: Process

Forward Relation: involves2

Reverse Relation: isInvolvedIn2

Supertypes: Involves

5.19. Manifests

Attributes: InverseFunctional, Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: DevelopedElement

Target: DevelopmentState

Forward Relation: manifests A DevelopmentalElement e has manifests a DevelopmentState s if and only if e attains s during its development process.

Reverse Relation: isManfestedBy See manifests .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.20. Prepares

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Role

Target: Product

Forward Relation: prepares

Reverse Relation: isPreparedBy

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement, HasAssignment

5.21. Produces

Attributes: InverseFunctional, Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Authority

Target: Product

Forward Relation: produces A WorkPackage w produces a Product p if and only if w is responsible for creation and development of p .

Reverse Relation: isProducedBy See produces .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.22. Realizes

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: RealizedElement

Target: RealizedElement

Forward Relation: realizes A SpecifiedElement c 1 realizes a SpecifiedElement c 2 if and only if c 1 is proffered by a supplier as a specification-compliant implementation of c 2 .

Reverse Relation: isRealizedBy See realizes .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.23. Receives

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: InterchangePoint

Target: Deliverable

Forward Relation: receives A Process p receives a Deliverable r if and only if the successful execution of p is contingent on the receipt of r .

Reverse Relation: isReceivedBy See receives .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, AssignedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.24. ReliesOn

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Authority

Target: DevelopedElement

Forward Relation: reliesOn

Reverse Relation: isReliedOnBy

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, AssignedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.25. Represents

Attributes: Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Stakeholder

Target: Concern

Forward Relation: represents A Stakeholder s represents Concern c if and only if s has a direct, legitimate, and recognized interest in c as it relates to a particular Project or Program .

Reverse Relation: isRepresentedBy See represents .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

5.26. Supplies

Attributes: InverseFunctional, Asymmetric, Irreflexive

Source: Authority

Target: SuppliedElement

Forward Relation: supplies A WorkPackage w supplies a Component c if and only if w is responsible for the acquisition and delivery of c .

Reverse Relation: isSuppliedBy See supplies .

Supertypes: analysis:AnalyzedElement, AssignedElement, analysis:CharacterizedElement

7. Scalar Properties

7.1. isRequiredNotLaterThan

A ScheduledElement e isRequiredNotLaterThan d if and only if e must be completed or achieved no later than d .

Domain: Deliverable

Scalar value type: date

Attributes: Functional
