Class project:Authority

dc:description

An [=Authority=] is any entity that asserts a fact in a model. Example [=Authority=]s include [=Program=], [=Project=], and [=WorkPackage=]. (The association of an [=Authority=] with its asserted facts requires mechanisms outside the vocabulary of OML and OWL proper, e.g., named graphs. Authorization is conveyed by the organization of statements in a repository.)
